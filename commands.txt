Available Commands:

1. Time - Tells the current time.
2. Wikipedia - Searches Wikipedia for information.
3. Send Email - Sends an email.
4. Search in Chrome - Opens a search query in Chrome.
5. Logout - Logs out the current user.
6. Shutdown - Shuts down the computer.
7. Restart - Restarts the computer.
8. Play Song - Plays a song.
9. Remember That - Remembers a piece of information.
10. Do You Know Anything - Retrieves the remembered information.
11. Screenshot - Takes a screenshot.
12. CPU - Provides information about CPU usage and battery.
13. Date - Tells the current date.
14. Offline - Quits the program.
